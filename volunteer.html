Get Involved

Volunteer with the Gibby Foundation

Join our community of passionate volunteers who help make basketball accessible to youth in our neighborhood. Your time and skills can make a real difference in young lives.

Coaching Assistant

Help coaches during practices and games. No experience required - we provide training.

  • • 2-4 hours per session
  • • Flexible scheduling
  • • Great for sports enthusiasts

Event Support

Assist with tournaments, clinics, and community events. Help with setup, registration, and coordination.

  • • Varies by event (2-6 hours)
  • • Fun, energetic environment
  • • Perfect for outgoing personalities

Transportation

Drive players to practices, games, and events. Valid license and clean driving record required.

  • • 1-2 hours per trip
  • • Background check required
  • • Flexible routes available

Meal Support

Help prepare and serve healthy meals and snacks for players before/after activities.

  • • 1-2 hours per session
  • • Kitchen experience helpful
  • • Great for nurturing types

Fundraising

Help organize community fundraisers, sponsorships, and grant applications.

  • • Flexible hours
  • • Business skills valuable
  • • Remote work possible

Youth Mentorship

Be a positive role model and mentor for our players. Share life experiences and guidance.

  • • 1-2 hours per week
  • • Background check required
  • • Life experience valued

Apply to Volunteer
