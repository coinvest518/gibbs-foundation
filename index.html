Programs

On-court training, off-court growth.

From beginners learning their first layup to varsity hopefuls, the Gibby Foundation runs year‑round experiences that develop skills, confidence, and character.

Scholarships available · No child turned away.

Junior Hoops Academy

Ages 8–12

Weekly fundamentals clinic focused on footwork, ball handling, teamwork, and fun games designed for newer players.

  • Saturday mornings · 10 weeks
  • Intro conditioning & confidence building

Suggested: free–$60 / season

Future Stars League

Ages 13–16

A community league with team practices, refs, jerseys, and playoffs — built on mentorship, leadership, and sportsmanship.

  • Two evenings per week · 12 week seasons
  • Mentor captains and post-game check-ins
  • Transportation support available

Pay-what-you-can · Full scholarships

College Pathway Clinics

Ages 16–18

Position-specific workouts, film study, and academic support for athletes exploring next‑level opportunities.

  • Skill labs · film review · guest coaches
  • Academic check-ins & college nights

Limited capacity · Invite + referral

About

A foundation born from a teammate's legacy.

The Gibby Foundation honors the life of "Gibby" — a teammate who never stopped lifting others up. We carry that energy into every practice, every huddle, and every high‑five.

Our volunteers include former players, teachers, social workers, and neighbors who believe that structured play, mentorship, and safety should be standard for every young person, not a privilege.
